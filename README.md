# 📊 E-commerce Sales & Customer Analysis Dashboard

## 🔍 Overview

This project is an interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze e-commerce sales data and understand customer behavior, revenue trends, and business performance.

The dashboard provides insights into:

* Revenue performance over time
* Customer segmentation
* Product performance
* Impact of discounts and payment methods

---

## 📁 Dataset

The dataset includes transactional data with fields such as:

* Transaction ID
* Customer ID
* Product Category
* Quantity, Price, Discount
* Transaction Date
* Payment Method
* Customer demographics (Age, Gender, Location, Income Group, Loyalty Score)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 🟦 Page 1 — Overview

* Total Revenue
* Total Transactions
* Total Customers
* Revenue by Month
* Revenue by Product Category
* Revenue by Customer Location

---

### 🟩 Page 2 — Customer Analysis

* Revenue by Customer Age Group
* Revenue by Customer Gender
* Revenue by Customer Income Group
* Revenue by Customer Loyalty Score

---

### 🟨 Page 3 — Business Insights

* Revenue by Payment Method
* Discount vs Revenue Analysis
* Revenue by Customer Location (Top areas)

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Middle-aged customers generate the highest revenue
* Revenue is fairly consistent across months
* Product categories like Clothing and Electronics perform strongly
* Discounts tend to slightly reduce overall revenue
* Customer loyalty has a positive impact on spending
